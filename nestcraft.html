🧠 The Bigger Picture

What the numbers can't capture:
• Esquire = premium lifestyle upgrade — better society, amenities, newer construction, social circle. This has real value beyond ₹.
• Sherwood = financial breathing room — more vacations, more SIPs, less EMI stress. But it's an older society and you may feel the "itch to upgrade" in 3-5 years.
• Rent → Buy Later = maximum flexibility today, but you're betting that Esquire prices won't run away AND that you'll maintain discipline to invest the surplus.

The honest question: If Sherwood + heavy investing gives you ₹X more in 8 years, is that ₹X worth more to you than living in Esquire for those 8 years? Only you and Neha can answer that.
